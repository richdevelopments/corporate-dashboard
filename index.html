Instructions:

Selete a metric from the dropdown menu to see each regions performance for that metric throughout the year. To analyse the data in a more specific time period in the year, drag over the Timeline chart to select the desired time in the year. The Stacked Chart will then zoom in to that time period, displaying the results for all 4 regions. The Bar charts on the right will display an average of the performance for that time period selected and the Pie chart will display the company size, which you can hover over to see the exact units per slice.
